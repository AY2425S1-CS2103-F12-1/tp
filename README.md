# inSUREance app

### Welcome to the inSUREance app, a one-stop shop for insurance agents like you

Finding it hard to keep tracks of who's who, or what they have purchased? Fret not for 
inSUREance is sure to get the job done. With features including:

- Adding plans to clients
- Add tags to clients (insurance type, customer profile)
- And many more!

You can work hard with a peace of mind while inSUREance works even harder for you :)
